Important Information

  • All the bookings at NIEGH/PGH are to be done well in advance.
  • All those availing the Guest House/PG Hostel are requested to bring and submit a photocopy of their respective proof/ documents/ PPO, etc. along with original ID proof for verification purpose at the time of their check in.
  • The checkout time is 12:00 Noon.
  • Manager NIE-GH reserves the right not to provide accommodation facilities in the Guest House/ PG Hostel or summarily evict the guest in the absence of a valid proof of identity or in the event of violation of any of the rules and regulation of the Guest House.
  • It should be ensured by the Guest before leaving the Guest House that all payments have been made and the key of the room has been handed over to the counter.
  • Guests going out of the room should place the key of the room on the keyboard at the counter.
  • Collection of Rent on working days (i.e., Monday to Friday) shall be done only between 9.30 a.m. to 4.30 p.m.
  • Mess charges have to be paid separately to the Mess Contractor.
  • Only bed-tea will be served in the room. Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner will be served in the dining hall.
  • Consumption of alcohol is strictly prohibited in the Guest House
